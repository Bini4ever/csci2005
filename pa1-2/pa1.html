About ME

I like a lot of activities including sport: soccer, basketball, biking; video games; TV-shows such as: the walking dead, game of throne, American horror story… However, my most favorite hobby is photography. I love art, and I really enjoy taking pictures. When I take an interesting and nice photo, I feel so good, joyful and satisfied. I found photography as a way of relaxing when I stressed.

I am not professionally experienced photographer, but I consider my style as Art photography, who uses photography to express himself or to express the way he visual things. Mostly I like taking picture of natures, landscapes, a little object any thing that looks interesting for me. I got my camera three years ago, but I broke it this summer in South Dakota for camping trip. Now I only use my phone to take pictures, and I have 3 lenses that I use to guide me with focusing. They are very cheaper and interesting.

In my opinion, to be a good photographer, you have to know 3 basic skills. One of them is light; you have to know where the light is coming from and your position. Your position should be where the light is coming from. The second one is angle; you need to choose the best angle that makes the object visually interesting. The third one is focus; focus is basically what lens type you use in order to get a clear image. For example if you want to take a picture of a tiny object very closely, you should use macro-lens; if you want to focus on far-away objects you can use telephoto lens; and if you want an interesting-wide angle view, you can use fisheye. Over all photography is my most favorite activity.

Yeah! I love Photography. Unfortunately, I’m majoring computer sciences, and its mostly indoor, so by keep doing photography in my free time, I believe I would be more happier in my future life.
